About Us

OUR FOUNDER SAYS

Every human being in this world is endowed with inherent
humanitarian warmth, which can be brought out to benefit others.
All it would need is favourable ambience to stimulate them. The
resultant exuding warmth will go on to become each one's identity
or address. Mugavari (means 'ADDRESS' in Tamil) endeavours to
achieve this lofty goal by imparting higher education to one and all.
